# Responsible Social Media Use – Do's and Don'ts (For College Students)

##  Do's

- **Think before you post**  
  Make sure your content is appropriate and won’t harm your reputation.

- **Use strong privacy settings**  
  Limit who can see your posts and personal information.

- **Verify information before sharing**  
  Check facts to avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.

- **Respect others online**  
  Be polite and avoid abusive language or harassment.

- **Report suspicious or harmful content**  
  Use platform tools to report scams, fake accounts, or cyberbullying.

---

##  Don'ts

- **Don’t share personal or sensitive information**  
  Avoid posting phone numbers, addresses, or financial details.

- **Don’t accept requests from unknown people**  
  Fake profiles are often used for scams or data theft.

- **Don’t click on suspicious links**  
  These may lead to phishing websites or malware.

- **Don’t engage in online arguments or bullying**  
  It can escalate quickly and have serious consequences.

- **Don’t overshare your daily activities or location**  
  This can put your safety at risk.
