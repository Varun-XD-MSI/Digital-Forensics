# Case Study: UPI Payment Fraud in India

UPI payment fraud is an issue in India. Lots of people get tricked into sending money or sharing information. This usually happens because someone is trying to trick them not because the person doing it is a skilled computer expert.

In a situation a college student gets a message or call saying they have won something or need to get some money. The person trying to scam them sends a request on a UPI app like Google Pay or PhonePe. The student is told to enter their UPI PIN to get the money.. Instead money gets taken out of their account. Sometimes fake links are also sent that look like payment pages and people enter their details without realizing the danger.

The main people who get targeted are students people looking for jobs and people who're not very familiar with digital payments. The people trying to scam them use tricks like saying they have to act or making them excited or scared to manipulate them.

The results can be very bad. People can lose a lot of money in a few minutes. Since UPI payments happen away it is hard to get the money back. The people who get scammed may also feel very stressed. Lose trust in online payment systems.

These cases show how important it is to be careful when using payments. People should never share their UPI PIN. Approve payments, from people they do not know.

If someone gets scammed they can report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in). Call 1930.
